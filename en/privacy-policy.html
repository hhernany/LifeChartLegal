LifeDash

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Summary

LifeDash does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All data remains exclusively on your device and, optionally, in your personal iCloud account.

1. Data collected

LifeDash does not collect any personal data. The app works entirely offline and does not require login, registration, a user account, or any form of identification.

No information is sent to external servers, third-party APIs, analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising networks.

2. Data storage

All data you create in LifeDash — such as habits, checklists, workouts, reminders, and trackers — is stored locally on your device using Apple's native SwiftData framework.

We have no access to any of this data. It belongs exclusively to you.

3. iCloud sync

If you choose to use sync, your data will be synchronized across your Apple devices through iCloud (CloudKit), a service provided and managed by Apple Inc.

In this case:

  • Data is transmitted directly from your device to Apple's iCloud servers, without passing through any of our servers.
  • Encryption and protection of data in transit and at rest is Apple's responsibility.
  • The developer of LifeDash has no access to data stored in your iCloud.

To learn more about how Apple handles your data, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Subscriptions and payments

LifeDash offers subscription plans processed entirely through Apple's native in-app purchase system (StoreKit / App Store). No financial data — such as credit card numbers, bank details, or billing information — is collected, processed, or stored by LifeDash.

All payment management, renewals, and cancellations are handled by Apple. See Apple Media Services Terms for more information.

5. Children's data

LifeDash does not collect data from any user, including minors. Since no personal information is collected or transmitted, the app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and equivalent regulations.

6. Third-party services

LifeDash does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data. We do not use:

  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs

The only external integration is with Apple's iCloud, as described in section 3.

7. Your rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account, you have full control over it. You can:

  • View all your data directly in the app.
  • Edit or delete any data at any time within the app.
  • Erase all iCloud data through the app's Settings section.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data.

There is no need to request data deletion from the developer, as none of your data is in our possession.

8. Changes to this policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be published on this same page with the updated revision date. We recommend checking this page periodically.

If there are significant changes to how we handle data — for example, if we begin collecting any type of information — we will notify users through the app before the change takes effect.
